About

Boy oh boy, we sure do love cards. Greeting cards, debit cards, credit cards, membership cards, trading cards. We love them all, and we sure would like to help you also love cards. Big cards, small cards, green cards, yellow cards. Cards of all types. Those are all of the types of cards that we have just listed. I could go on and on about how much I like cards all day. They are the best when they are rectangular but they can also be other shapes like circle and tesseract. Cards sure are cool. The only things that come close to how cool cards are would be crabs. Crabs are pretty cool too.

Who could forget crabs? They’re like cards but with less paper and more claws. Clickety clack. That is the sound that their crabby claws make when they snap their fingers at crab poetry night. And by fingers I mean claws, because crabs have those. Crabs also like poetry. We can never know for sure but I bet William Wordsworth and also William Shakespeare were definitely both crabs. When crabs write poetry, they write it on cards and send them to other crabs. It all comes full circle. It’s cards and crabs all the way down.
